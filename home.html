"Contestants Line Up!"

Valentino Rossi

Valentino Rossi is a renowned Italian racing icon, popularly known as The Doctor, with an incredible career in MotoGP. A nine-time Grand Prix motorcycle racing World Champion, he holds numerous records for premier class victories and podium finishes. Rossi has triumphed with both Honda and Yamaha, dominating the 2000s era. After a brief stint with Ducati, he made a successful comeback with Yamaha, consistently achieving outstanding results.

Francesco Bagnaia

Francesco "Pecco" Bagnaia is a highly accomplished Italian motorcycle racer who competes in MotoGP for the Ducati Lenovo Team. He won the MotoGP Riders' World Championship in 2022 and 2023, following his victory in the Moto2 World Championship in 2018. Notably, Bagnaia is the first rider from Valentino Rossi's VR46 Riders Academy to claim a world title in the premier class. His achievements include breaking a 15-year World Championship drought for Ducati riders and becoming the first Italian since Rossi in 2009 to secure the title.

Dani Pedrosa

Dani Pedrosa Ramal is a retired Spanish Grand Prix motorcycle racer who was a three-time World Champion in the 125cc and 250cc categories. He currently works as a test and development rider for Red Bull KTM Factory Racing. Pedrosa has raced for KTM in 2021 and 2023, achieving notable results. He is highly regarded for his successful stint with the Repsol Honda Team in MotoGP, finishing as runner-up in the championship on multiple occasions.
